About the Competition

The Al-Izza Qur'an Recitation Competition invites participants from all backgrounds to join us in honoring the beauty and teachings of the Qur'an. Scholarships and awards await those who show exceptional dedication and skill.

AL-IZZA 7TH ANNUAL MUSAABAQAH

Competition Details:

Musaabaqah Preliminary: Friday 29th (After Jumaat Service) to Saturday 30th.

Grand Finale and Prize Presentation: Sunday 1st December, 8am.

How to Register:

Follow the steps below to register for the Al-Izza 7th Annual Musaabaqah:

  1. Click on the registration link below to open the registration form.
